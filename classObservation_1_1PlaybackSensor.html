bool Observation::PlaybackSensor::Poll ( ) [protected, virtual]

Note that the Poll() method operates slightly differently in the PlaybackSensor. The first call to poll actually starts the playback and returns the value at line 1 of the file. Subsequent calls to Poll() establish the current time and seek to the appropriate line of the file.
